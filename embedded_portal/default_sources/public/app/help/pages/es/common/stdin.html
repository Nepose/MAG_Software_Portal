Entering data

The remote is the main tool for data entry.

The group of programmable buttons is meant for controlling other devices. See the First launch guide supplied with the device to learn how to assign control functions to the buttons.

Please note that functions of coloured buttons and some other hot keys are covered in apps (see the bottom service panel) and in the side modal menu. Their tasks may differ depending on a current app.
