Online Media

The Online Media app offers access to device-integrated online services: paid and free Ukrainian, Russian and international TV channels, online radio, media libraries of VoD and ОТТ content providers. An actual list depends on geographic location of a user and a device. The service list is updated regularly.

To display short info on every service, select (hover the cursor over) the menu item, then press the or button on the remote. Terms of use for online cinemas vary depending on content type, creation date and relevancy. To learn more about rates and tariffs, consult official web sites of online cinemas.
